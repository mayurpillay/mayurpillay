Filterbird

This idea came to me when I was thinking about how Twitter would be more useful to me. At the time (2-3 years ago) Tweets weren't really being sorted and categorized. I knew that although difficult, this could be possible through machine learning and natural language processing algorithms. The project gave me the chance to create a design for a Twitter client for Mac and the icons to go with it. Now Twitter does things like "Top Tech Tweets", which are algorithmically selected.

About Me

I like interesting design and product challenges. I'm passionate about creating beautiful, functional user interfaces and the requirements that go with them. I have worked as a product manager and like tying in those skills with my design sensibilities. I didn't really start off as a web designer, but was given an opportunity and continued with it.
