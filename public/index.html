  • Lasso.fm

    I designed and managed the implementation of the Lasso.fm website. Lasso.fm is a startup working in the live music space. I faced some challenges with this design, including coming up with a site that would appeal to musicians and artists as well as promoters and possibly venues.

  • Zeppln

    Zeppln was a concept that I thought of one morning at 2am. I was actually sleeping and woke up to the idea of creating a site that would allow people to share anything that they create. I created a few mockups to solidify how the idea would work in practice.

  • The GCA Foundation (off site)

    A couple of fellow Tufts Alumni and I started the GCA Foundation to help underprivileged youth. It was our way of giving back. I designed and built our charity website and have been updating it since. It started out as a splash page that allowed donations and ticket buying. After a successful event, it has grown with us.

  • Filterbird

    A couple of years ago, Tweets weren't really categorized. I thought that the categorization of Tweets would be a useful bit of functionality, albeit challenging since the only way I could think of doing it would be to use machine learning and natural language processing algorithms. The idea took the form of some icons and an inspiration screen for a Twitter client for Mac.

  • Milkmade

    I connected with the founder of Milkmade, a craft ice cream company based in New York. At the time, she was looking to upgrade her website. I created a homepage inspiration screen with a chalkboard theme. The actual site took on more of luxury feel, but was a fun prompt.

  • Bears

    There is a story behind the bears at the top of the page. For a period of time, I really wanted to design a bear on a bicycle, I was intrigued by how fantastical it was, also I love animals. I found after taking the bear off the bicycle, that it was in sumo wrestler position, which might be infinitely cooler.

About Me

I like interesting design and product challenges. I'm passionate about creating beautiful, functional user interfaces and the requirements that go with them. I have worked as a product manager and like tying in those skills with my design sensibilities. I didn't really start off as a web designer, but was given an opportunity and continued with it.
